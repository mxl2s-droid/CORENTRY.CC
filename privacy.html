Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Overview

Corentry develops video editing and playback applications. This Privacy Policy explains how Corentry handles information when you use our apps, websites, and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as messages sent to support. Our apps may also process technical information such as device type, app version, operating system, crash logs, diagnostics, and basic usage events that help us maintain and improve app performance.

Photos, Videos, and Media Files

Video editing and playback apps may request access to photos, videos, audio, or files on your device so you can select, edit, preview, or play media. Unless a specific app feature clearly requires upload or sharing, media files are processed on your device and are not collected by Corentry.

Advertising and Analytics

Some apps may use advertising or analytics services to support the app, understand reliability, measure feature usage, or detect errors. These services may process identifiers, device information, approximate location, ad interaction data, or similar technical data according to their own privacy practices.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app functionality, respond to support requests, improve performance, fix issues, protect against misuse, comply with legal obligations, and maintain advertising or analytics integrations where applicable.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate apps, provide analytics, deliver ads, process diagnostics, or comply with legal requirements.

Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law or necessary to resolve disputes, enforce terms, or maintain security.

Children's Privacy

Corentry apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Your Choices

You can control device permissions such as photos, videos, camera, microphone, files, notifications, and tracking through your device settings. You may also contact us to request privacy-related assistance.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or Corentry apps, contact us at josephbradleysoladia@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
